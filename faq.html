About Myself
I am really eager to learn and I enjoy being organized. The field of computers and phones interest me because there is always room to learn. Everything is always changing.

What Are My Strengths?
I’d say my strengths are having a positive attitude and determination.

What Are My Greatest Weaknesses?
I’d say my biggest weaknesses are my fear of public speaking, harsh self-criticism, and working with a team.
Public Speaking
Harsh Self-Criticism
For me, if I am doing something I like to try to do it 110%. If I am going to take the time out to do it, I want to do it right instead of having to redo it. However, at times this can lead me to taking a little longer because I am always looking at how to make changes to make it better.
Working With a Team
It’s not the fact that I can’t get along with people because I like to believe that I can easily. I’d rather listen to others speak than speak myself. For me, it’s more that I don’t always trust that the other person will get the work done completely, to the best of their ability, or on time. I like to be self-sufficient.
